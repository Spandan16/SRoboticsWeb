About Us

  1. Home
  2. About Us

Spandan Robotics Pvt. Ltd. is an ISO 9001 - 2008 certified company. The company was formed in the year 2021 at Kalimpong, W.B, India. We mainly catered to training and consultancy services in the southern part of the country. We have trained more than 1200 students from Karnataka, Kerala and West Bengal. We have now shifted our entire operations to West Bengal. Our basic and core competencies lie in providing technical training services and consultancies to Professional Engineers, Engineering college students, OEM’s (Original Equipment Manufacturers) and service industries. After training almostseveral students from 18 different Colleges and Universities from the Southern and Eastern part of our country, we are now spreading our wings to our entire nation. Faculty member of Spandan Robotics are erstwhile scientists and engineers of ISRO, HAL & ADA in our country with more than 10 years’ experience. With our continuous endeavor towards providing high quality training services to Mechanical, Aeronautical and Aerospace engineers, we strive to bridge the gap between students and industry experts.
